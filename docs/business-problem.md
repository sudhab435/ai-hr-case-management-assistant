# Business Problem

HR teams manage high volumes of employee requests across payroll, benefits, leave, onboarding, and policy support. Many of these requests are repetitive and could be partially resolved through self-service or AI-assisted support.

Without automation, HR teams face:
- High manual workload
- Slow response times
- Inconsistent request routing
- Limited visibility into case trends
- Reduced capacity for strategic work

This project simulates an AI-enabled HR support workflow that improves intake, standardizes routing, and provides reporting visibility.
